Essential Japanese Phrases

  • Hello: こんにちは (Konnichiwa)
  • Thank you: ありがとう (Arigatou)
  • Yes: はい (Hai)
  • No: いいえ (Iie)
  • Excuse me: すみません (Sumimasen)
  • Where is...? ...はどこですか？ (...wa doko desu ka?)

Travel Tips

  • Use public transportation for efficient travel between cities.
  • Respect local customs and etiquette, such as removing shoes before entering homes.
  • Stay hydrated and carry a portable charger for your devices.
